WHAT'S NEW

  • Online classes for students resuming from 10th June, 2021

    Virtual Online Classes for the school students are resuming from 10th June, 2021. The summer vaccations are over. Due to the pandemic situation, offline classes haven't been started.

  • Model United Nations (MUN) for class 6th and 7th students coming up on 19th and 20th June, 2021

    Model United Nations (MUN) is a platform for students to represent a country and share its point of view on a particular agenda. They get a chance to speak, perform and get question-answer round where the other delegates ask questions to the one who is speaking.

Shikhar Dhawan

ACHIEVEMENTS

HT Pace Award - (February 2020)

Aashvi Ahlawat and Manya Jain of Class VII-A participated in HT Pace Contest Quiz No. 608 and 609 respectively in the month of February, 2020. The quiz was sponsored by Penguin Books. The results were declared the following week. Manya won the second prize and Aashvi won the consolation prize in their respective quiz. They were handed over the prizes by the Vice Principal, Ms. R. Anand.

Brainfeed School Excellence Award - (2020)

We receive the prestigious School Excellence Award from Brainfeed Magazine. The award is in recognition to our outstanding education practices and our contribution in creating global leaders. We are applauded for our innovative and creative practices, sports education, co-curricular activities and much more. The award is also for our innovative approach and committed efforts in grooming young learners with skills for life.

Zamit Award - (2020)

Ms. S. Arora, Ms. M. Som, Ms. S. Kaur and Ms. S. Suresh of St. Mark’s Sr. Sec. Public School, Meera Bagh received the 8th Zamit International School Awards at the 11th EDU ConvEx organised by Planet Edu. The conference included many talks about Learning Power by some eminent speakers like Mr. John Kerr (former CEO of Edexcel Foundation), Prof. M. M. Pant (Founder, LMP Education Trust), Mr. Sanjay Malviya (Managing Trustee, The Future Foundation) and Mr. Sanjeev Sehgal (COO – Planet EDU Exams).
